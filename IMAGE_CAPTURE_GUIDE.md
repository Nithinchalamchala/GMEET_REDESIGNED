# 📸 Visual Assets Capture Guide
> *A precise guide to generating the perfect UI screenshots required for your `end_semester_report.tex`.*

To guarantee your End-Semester HCI Portfolio looks flawlessly executed, we designed the LaTeX report to embed your real, high-fidelity prototypes. Because the AI vision backend was temporarily down, you get to take the screenshots! This guarantees your screenshots will perfectly match your local screen resolution.

Below is the exact step-by-step walkthrough detailing how to spawn each intricate UI component so you can take a screenshot (`Cmd + Shift + 4` on your Macbook) and save them to your `images/` directory.

---

### 1. The Contextual Meeting Assistant (`assistant_bubble.png`)
**Location:** Main Meeting Interface 
**Trigger Instructions:**
1. Open up `index.html` in your browser.
2. Join the meeting.
3. **Do absolutely nothing.** Wait on the screen for about **8 seconds** without moving your mouse or clicking.
4. The anthropological Meeting Assistant bubble will smoothly animate in from the bottom corner saying *"I noticed you haven't spoken..."*
5. 📸 Take your screenshot and save it as `images/assistant_bubble.png`!

### 2. The Breathing Exercise Overlay (`breathing_ring.png`)
**Location:** Main Meeting Interface
**Trigger Instructions:**
1. Inside `index.html` (the active meeting), locate the bottom control bar.
2. Click the **"More Options"** button (the vertical triple dots or menu icon).
3. In the supplementary menu that pops up, click the option labeled **"Take a Breathe"**.
4. A large, beautifully animated, pulsing radial gradient overlay will cover the grid. 
5. 📸 Take your screenshot while the ring expands and save it as `images/breathing_ring.png`!

### 3. Breakout Rooms Panel (`breakout_panel.png`)
**Location:** Main Meeting Interface
**Trigger Instructions:**
1. Inside `index.html`, return to the bottom control bar.
2. Click the **"More Options"** button again.
3. Select **"Breakout Rooms"**.
4. A large side-panel will slide open smoothly from the right, dynamically sorting 'Participants' into partitioned geometric cards via Gestalt Grouping.
5. 📸 Take your screenshot of the panel and save it as `images/breakout_panel.png`!

### 4. Focus Mode Engaged (`focus_active.png`)
**Location:** Main Meeting Interface
**Trigger Instructions:**
1. Look at your bottom control bar in `index.html`.
2. Locate the **"Focus Mode"** button (this is usually represented by an eye icon next to the hand raise).
3. Click it to toggle the mode active.
4. Notice how the user interface dynamically responds to Tesler's Law by aggressively dimming the secondary layout cards and focusing solely on the active container. 
5. 📸 Capture this high-contrast state and save it as `images/focus_active.png`!

### 5. Adaptive Verification Challenge (`adaptive_captcha.png`)
**Location:** Dashboard Onboarding
**Trigger Instructions:**
1. Open up `dashboard.html` in your browser.
2. Select your persona and follow the setup wizard to the final step.
3. Click the **"Join Meeting"** button.
4. An "Evaluating Security..." heuristic spinner will run, and then the **Motor-Kinetic Spatial Slider** (Slide the key to the lock) will effortlessly slide up.
5. 📸 Quickly capture this screenshot while it is visible and save it as `images/adaptive_captcha.png`!

### 6. Universal Accessibility: Motor-Assist Tracker (`motor_assist.png`)
**Location:** Main Meeting Interface 
**Trigger Instructions:**
1. Open up `index.html` in your browser.
2. In the bottom toolbar, open the **"More Options"** menu (the vertical triple dots).
3. Click the option labeled **"Accessibility: Motor-Assist Tracker"**.
4. The moment you click it, the bottom toolbar will invoke Fitts's Law. It will drastically scale all operational buttons to maximum width, apply heavy negative space padding, and bold text layouts to prevent tremor-based misclicks.
5. 📸 Take your screenshot encompassing the massive bottom control bar and save it as `images/motor_assist.png`!
